## Probability Distribution: When Does the Law Take Effect?

*Methodology: based on constitutional procedure timelines, precedent from Oct 2025 round (14 days redação final), Seguro's political alignment (PS), PS statements post-vote.*

*Rev 2 (3 Apr): PS says nationality law constitutional issues "resolved" (Delgado Alves, 1 Apr 21:02). Only evaluating fiscalização on Penal Code decree (loss of nationality). Two decrees are separate — nationality law can proceed independently. This shifts probability mass from TC scenarios to direct promulgation/veto. Seguro (PS) could still refer to TC independently, but unlikely to contradict his own party's constitutional assessment.*
